Case studies

We’ll be adding case studies to help you understand how other HMRC teams have reused APIs and file transfer patterns to get data or transfer data across HMRC.

By reusing what’s already available, HMRC teams don’t need to build a single use solution which:

  • saves time and money
  • improves flexibility, governance, speed and usability
  • encourages change and innovation
  • removes the need for single use APIs or file transfers
